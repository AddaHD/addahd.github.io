About Us

Mens Den is a mens peer support group set up in May 2018. We offer a safe place that is non-judgemental and open to men of all ages, regardless of race, disability etc.

We meet at Colne Open Door, Wednesday evening between 6pm and 7:30pm.

The format for our meeting is simple:

  • Chick-in - Gives each chap a few minutes to talk about their week; the good,the bad and whats on their mind
  • Chat - Opportunity to grab a coffee and chat about things of interest with others
  • Chick-out - Opportunity to sum up how they are feeling and they plan to do in the week coming

We also run a private WhatsApp group available to those who attend the group. This gives each chap additional support away from our weekly meeting should they find themselves is a spot of difficulty. We are like a Band of Brothers, there for each other.
